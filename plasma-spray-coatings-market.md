<p><p>Plasma spray coatings refer to a technique used to apply protective or decorative coatings onto various materials. It involves heating a high-temperature gas (plasma) to ionize it, followed by injecting a powdered coating material into the plasma stream. The molten particles of the coating material are then propelled towards the target material, where they solidify and adhere, forming a dense and protective coating.</p><p>The plasma spray coatings market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue its upward trajectory.
