<p><p>The Automotive Steering-by-Wire System market is segmented into four types: mechanical steering (MS), hydraulic power steering (HPS), electric hydraulic power steering (EHPS), and electric power steering (EPS). Mechanical steering systems are traditional and rely on physical force, while hydraulic power steering uses fluid pressure to assist in steering. EHPS combines the benefits of hydraulic and electric power steering, providing efficient energy use. Electric power steering eliminates the need for fluid, using an electric motor to assist in the steering process. Each type has its strengths and weaknesses, catering to different automotive needs.</p></p>
