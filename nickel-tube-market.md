<p><p>The nickel tube market can be categorized into two main types: pure nickel tube and alloy nickel tube market. Pure nickel tube refers to tubes made solely from nickel without any other elements. These tubes are used in applications that require high corrosion resistance and excellent thermal conductivity. On the other hand, alloy nickel tubes are made by combining nickel with other elements like copper or chromium, which enhances specific properties such as strength, durability, or resistance to extreme temperatures. These tubes find applications in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and chemical processing.</p></p>
