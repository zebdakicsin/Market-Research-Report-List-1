<p><strong>The Metal Ion Chelator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Precipitation</li><li>Complex</li><li>Phosphates</li><li>Organic Polyphosphoric Acids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal ion chelator market comprises various types such as precipitation, complex, phosphates, and organic polyphosphoric acids. Precipitation chelators eliminate metal ions by forming insoluble precipitates. Complex chelators chemically bind with metal ions to form stable complexes. Phosphates chelators sequester metal ions by converting them into insoluble compounds. Organic polyphosphoric acids bind with metal ions to form soluble complexes and prevent them from precipitating. These different types of chelators are used in various industries to remove or control metal ions in processes such as water treatment, metal finishing, and pharmaceutical manufacturing.</p></p>
