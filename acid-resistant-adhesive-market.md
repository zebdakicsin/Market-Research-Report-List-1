<p><p>The Acid Resistant Adhesive market consists of two types: One-component and Two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are pre-mixed and ready to use directly from the container, eliminating the need for additional mixing or preparation. They offer convenience and simplicity in application. On the other hand, Two-component adhesives require the mixing of two different components, usually a base resin and a hardener, before application.
