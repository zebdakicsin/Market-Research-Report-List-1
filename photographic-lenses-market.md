<p><p>Photographic lenses are categorized into various market types based on their specific functions and features. </p><p>Long lenses, also known as telephoto lenses, have a significantly longer focal length, allowing photographers to capture distant subjects in great detail or achieve a shallow depth of field. </p><p>Macro lenses are designed to capture incredibly close-up images with high magnification, ideal for photographing small subjects such as insects or flowers, achieving stunning levels of detail. </p><p>Special-purpose lenses encompass a wide range of lenses designed for specific photography applications, such as fisheye lenses for wide-angle distortion or tilt-shift lenses for perspective control. </p><p>Lastly, other lenses include a diverse array of options catering to various needs, such as wide-angle lenses, standard lenses, or zoom lenses, offering versatile shooting capabilities.</p></p>
