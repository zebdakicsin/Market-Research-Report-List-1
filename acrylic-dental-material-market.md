<p><p>The acrylic dental material market consists of three main types: powder, gel, and liquid. Powder is a solid substance finely ground into small particles that are mixed with liquid to form a paste-like material for dental applications. Gel, on the other hand, is a semi-solid substance that has a thick and sticky consistency. It is used in dental procedures to provide cushioning and absorb shock. Liquid refers to a fluid material that is combined with powder or gel to create a smooth and workable consistency for dental purposes.</p></p>
