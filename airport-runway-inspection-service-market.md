<p><p>Airport runway inspection service market can be divided into two types: high altitude inspection and ground inspection. High altitude inspection involves the assessment of the runway from an aerial perspective using drones or helicopters. This method allows for a comprehensive view of the entire runway surface and any potential hazards. On the other hand, ground inspection involves physically examining the runway surface by ground personnel. This method focuses on identifying any signs of deterioration, such as cracks or foreign objects, that could pose a risk to aircraft operations. Both types of inspection are crucial for maintaining the safety and efficiency of airport runways.</p></p>
