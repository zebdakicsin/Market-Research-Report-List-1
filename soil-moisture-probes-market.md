<p><p>Soil moisture probes are used in agriculture to measure the moisture content in the soil. Capacitance type probes work by measuring changes in electrical capacitance, which vary with soil moisture levels. They are highly accurate and can be used in various soil types. Resistor type probes measure the resistance of the soil, which is influenced by the moisture content. They are simpler in design and less expensive than capacitance type probes. Both types of probes are widely used in the market to help farmers optimize irrigation and prevent over or under watering.</p></p>
