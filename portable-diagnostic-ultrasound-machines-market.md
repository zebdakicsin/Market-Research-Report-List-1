<p><p>Portable Diagnostic Ultrasound Machines are medical devices used to visualize and monitor different parts of the body. A-mode refers to displaying amplitude variations to assess tissue density. B-mode utilizes brightness variations to create cross-sectional images. C-mode provides real-time images and is commonly used for eye exams. F-mode is used for fetal monitoring during pregnancy. M-mode allows the measurement of movement over time and is helpful in assessing cardiac function. These various modes cater to different diagnostic needs and play a significant role in the Portable Diagnostic Ultrasound Machines market.</p></p>
