<p><ul><li>Pretreatment Agent</li><li>Electroplating Additive</li><li>Post-treatment Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electroplating chemistry market consists of three main types: pretreatment agents, electroplating additives, and post-treatment agents. Pretreatment agents are used to clean and prepare the surface of the object before electroplating, ensuring better adhesion of the plating material. Electroplating additives help modify the plating process by improving the adhesion, brightness, and hardness of the plated material. Post-treatment agents are used to enhance the appearance and corrosion resistance of the plated surface, providing a protective layer and ensuring long-lasting results.</p></p>
