<p><p>Yaw damper is a device used in aircraft to reduce yawing oscillations. Yawing is a motion in which the aircraft's nose swings side to side. This can happen due to several factors such as turbulence, engine failure, or control inputs. Yaw dampers help stabilize the aircraft by automatically applying corrective inputs to the rudder, counteracting the yaw motion.</p><p>The current outlook for the yaw damper market is positive and expected to grow significantly in the coming years. The increasing demand for aircraft, both commercial and military, is one of the major factors driving the market growth. As air travel continues to rise, the need for advanced safety systems like yaw dampers becomes crucial to ensure a smooth and stable flight.</p><p>Furthermore, the implementation of stringent safety regulations by aviation authorities is also boosting market growth. Yaw dampers help improve aircraft stability, reduce pilot workload, and enhance passenger comfort, making them an essential component of modern aircraft.</p><p>Technological advancements and innovations in yaw damper systems are also contributing to market growth.
