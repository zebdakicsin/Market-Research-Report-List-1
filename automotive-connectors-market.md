<p><p>Automotive Connectors Market consists of three major types: Wire to Wire Connector, Wire to Board Connector, and Board to Board Connector. A Wire to Wire Connector enables electrical connection between two separate wires in a vehicle's electrical system. A Wire to Board Connector connects wires to a circuit board, enabling communication and power transmission. Lastly, a Board to Board Connector establishes a connection between two circuit boards, allowing the transfer of electrical signals and data. These connectors play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and reliable electrical connections within automotive systems.</p></p>
