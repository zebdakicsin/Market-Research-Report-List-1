<p><p>The Overhead Railway Electrification System refers to the infrastructure that powers trains through an electric supply suspended above the railway tracks. This system is commonly used in urban and suburban rail networks to provide an efficient and environmentally friendly mode of transport.</p><p>The market analysis of the Overhead Railway Electrification System indicates a significant growth potential in the coming years.
