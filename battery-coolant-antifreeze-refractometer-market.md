<p><p>The battery coolant-antifreeze refractometer market encompasses two main types: optical refractometers and digital refractometers. Optical refractometers use the principle of light refraction to measure the refractive index of the coolant-antifreeze solution. They require manual interpretation of the scale to determine the concentration of coolant-antifreeze. On the other hand, digital refractometers provide a digital reading of the refractive index, making them more precise and easier to use. These instruments play a crucial role in maintaining the appropriate concentration of coolant-antifreeze in batteries, ensuring their optimal performance and longevity.</p></p>
