<p><p>The flexible frozen food packaging market includes various types such as bags, pouches, wraps, lidding, interleaving, vacuum skin packaging, and brick packs. Bags and pouches are popular options for storing and preserving frozen food, while wraps provide a convenient way to package individual portions. Lidding is used for sealing trays or cups containing frozen food. Interleaving involves placing a layer between frozen food items to prevent them from sticking together. Vacuum skin packaging uses a tight film to create a vacuum seal around the food, while brick packs are rectangular packages ideal for frozen food storage.</p></p>
