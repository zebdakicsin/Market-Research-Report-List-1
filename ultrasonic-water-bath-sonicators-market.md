<p><p>Ultrasonic water bath sonicators are used for various applications like sample preparation, cell lysis, and emulsification. Circulating water baths are equipped with a built-in pump that continuously circulates the water, ensuring uniform temperature distribution throughout the bath. This feature enables better temperature control, making them ideal for precise and sensitive experiments. On the other hand, non-circulating water baths do not have this pump, but they still provide consistent heating for general laboratory use. Both types of water baths offer effective sonication capabilities for different research needs.</p></p>
