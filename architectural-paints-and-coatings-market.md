<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Architectural paints and coatings market types include water-borne, solvent-borne, and others. Water-borne paints and coatings are made with water as the main solvent. They are environmentally friendly, easy to clean, and have low levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Solvent-borne paints and coatings use organic solvents as the main solvent, providing excellent durability and resistance to wear and tear. However, they have higher VOC levels and are less environmentally friendly. Other types include powder coatings, which are applied as dry powder and then cured through heat, and radiation-curable coatings, which undergo a chemical reaction when exposed to UV light.</p></p>
