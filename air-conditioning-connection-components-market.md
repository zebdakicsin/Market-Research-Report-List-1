<p><p>The Air Conditioning Connection Components Market includes two main types: Split Components and Cabinet Components. Split Components refer to the various parts required for the installation of split air conditioning systems, such as outdoor unit, indoor unit, interconnecting pipes, and control devices. These components are designed to provide efficient cooling and are widely used in residential and commercial buildings. On the other hand, Cabinet Components are the parts used in cabinet air conditioning systems, including compressors, condensers, evaporators, and expansion valves. These components are usually larger in size and are suitable for industrial applications.</p></p>
