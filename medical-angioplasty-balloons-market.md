<p><p>The medical angioplasty balloon market includes various types of balloons used for different purposes. Normal balloons are simple balloons used to dilate narrowed blood vessels. Drug-eluting balloons are coated with medications to prevent re-narrowing of blood vessels. Cutting balloons have small blades on the surface to help cut through plaque inside the blood vessels. Scoring balloons have tiny scoring elements that create small grooves in the plaque to facilitate expansion. These different types of balloons provide options for medical professionals to choose the most suitable method for treating narrowed or blocked blood vessels during angioplasty procedures.</p></p>
