<p><p>NdFeB permanent magnets are widely used in various industries due to their strong magnetic properties. There are two main types in the market: bonded NdFeB and sintered NdFeB magnets. Bonded NdFeB magnets are made by mixing magnetic powder with a binder material and then compression molding it into desired shapes. They have lower magnetic properties but offer better dimensional accuracy and can be manufactured into complex shapes. Sintered NdFeB magnets are produced through a powder metallurgy process, resulting in magnets with higher magnetic properties but limited shapes and dimensions.</p></p>
