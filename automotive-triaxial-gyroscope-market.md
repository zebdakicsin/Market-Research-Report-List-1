<p><ul><li>Interferometric Gyroscope</li><li>Resonant Gyroscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Triaxial Gyroscope Market consists of two main types: the Interferometric Gyroscope and the Resonant Gyroscope. An interferometric gyroscope operates on the concept of interference patterns to measure rotation, utilizing the superposition principle of light waves. On the other hand, a resonant gyroscope utilizes the principle of resonant frequencies to measure rotation by detecting the changes in oscillation caused by rotation. Both types play a crucial role in the automotive industry, providing accurate and reliable measurements of rotation, which are essential for various applications such as vehicle stability control systems.</p></p>
