<p><strong>The Flip-Chip Package Substrate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FCBGA</li><li>FCCSP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flip-Chip Package Substrate (FCPKG) market includes two main types: Flip-Chip Ball Grid Array (FCBGA) and Flip-Chip Chip Scale Package (FCCSP). FCBGA involves mounting the chips directly on a substrate using solder balls for connection. It provides better electrical performance and thermal dissipation. On the other hand, FCCSP involves bonding the chips directly to a substrate without using solder balls. It offers a smaller form factor, improved electrical performance, and higher density compared to FCBGA. Both types are widely used in the semiconductor industry for various applications.</p></p>
