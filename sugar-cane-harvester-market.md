<p><p>The sugar cane harvester market consists of three main types: self-propelled, trailed, and mounted sugar cane harvesters. Self-propelled harvesters are equipped with their own power source and can move independently across the field for harvesting. Trailed harvesters are towed by another vehicle and are typically larger in size, allowing for higher efficiency and capacity. Mounted harvesters, on the other hand, are attached to tractors or similar vehicles and are suitable for smaller fields or areas with limited access. Each of these market types caters to different requirements and preferences in the sugar cane harvesting industry.</p></p>
