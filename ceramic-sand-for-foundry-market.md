Precoated sand offers enhanced casting surface quality by preventing defects like sand burn-in. Cold box sand utilizes a gaseous catalyst to harden the sand mixture, ensuring rapid curing and high strength. Self-hardening sand sets without external curing agents, yielding cost-effective castings. Lost foam casting employs ceramic sand as the mold material, allowing intricate shapes and smooth surface finishes. Lastly, other applications of ceramic sand in the foundry market include investment casting and shell molding processes.</p></p>
