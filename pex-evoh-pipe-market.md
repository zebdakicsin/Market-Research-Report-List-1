<p><p>PEX-EVOH pipe refers to a type of multilayer pipe that is commonly used in plumbing and heating systems. This pipe consists of several layers, including an inner layer made of cross-linked polyethylene (PEX) and an outer layer made of ethylene-vinyl alcohol (EVOH). The EVOH layer acts as an oxygen barrier, preventing the diffusion of oxygen into the pipe and reducing the risk of corrosion.</p><p>The market analysis of the PEX-EVOH pipe industry indicates a positive growth trajectory. <p><p>PEX-EVOH Pipe market consists of different types of pipes: PEX-A, PEX-B, PEX-C, and PEX-AL-PEX pipes. PEX-A pipes are highly flexible and resistant to bursting, making them suitable for retrofit applications. PEX-B pipes are slightly less flexible but offer excellent strength and affordability. PEX-C pipes are rigid and less flexible, primarily used for cold water applications. PEX-AL-PEX pipes combine the advantages of PEX and aluminum, providing improved strength and performance for both hot and cold water systems.</p></p>
