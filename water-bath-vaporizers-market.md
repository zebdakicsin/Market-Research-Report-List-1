<p><strong>The Water Bath Vaporizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indirect Fired Water Bath</li><li>Electric Water Bath</li><li>Steam Sparged Water Bath</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water bath vaporizers market comprises three types of vaporizers: indirect fired water bath, electric water bath, and steam sparged water bath. Indirect fired water bath vaporizers use a heat exchanger to transfer heat energy to the water bath, heating the process fluid indirectly. Electric water bath vaporizers have an electrical heating element submerged in the water, which directly heats the process fluid. Steam sparged water bath vaporizers inject steam into the water bath, heating the process fluid through direct contact with the steam. These vaporizers are widely used in various industries for vaporizing liquefied gases efficiently.</p></p>
