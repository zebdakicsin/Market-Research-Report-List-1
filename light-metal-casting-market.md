<p><p>Light metal casting is a manufacturing process used to produce complex shapes of various light metals such as aluminum, magnesium, and titanium. The increasing demand for lightweight materials in various end-use industries, especially automotive and aerospace, is one of the key factors driving market growth. The automotive industry, in particular, depends on light metal castings to reduce the weight of vehicles and improve fuel efficiency. The rising focus on reducing carbon emissions and increasing adoption of electric vehicles are further boosting the demand for light metal castings in the automotive sector.</p><p>Moreover, the growing construction industry, primarily in emerging economies, is also fueling market growth.
