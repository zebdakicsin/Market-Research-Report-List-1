<p><p>The vehicle oxygen sensor market comprises two main types: titanium oxide type and zirconia type. Titanium oxide sensors operate at high temperatures and are suitable for diesel engines, measuring the oxygen content in the exhaust gases. Zirconia sensors work at lower temperatures and are commonly used in gasoline engines. They measure the ratio between oxygen and fuel to ensure efficient combustion. Both types are essential in modern vehicles, helping to monitor and adjust the air and fuel mixture for optimal engine performance and reduced emissions.</p></p>
