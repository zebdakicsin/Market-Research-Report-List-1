<p><p>The HVAC valves market consists of various types of valves used in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems. These include ball valves, which have a spherical closure unit to control flow. Globe valves have a linear motion disc for regulating fluid flow. Butterfly valves use a disk-shaped closing mechanism to manage flow. Additionally, there are other valve types, such as gate valves and check valves, which are also commonly used in HVAC systems. Each valve type offers unique features and benefits, catering to different applications and requirements in the HVAC industry.</p></p>
