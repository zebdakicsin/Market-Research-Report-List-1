<p><p>Automotive Parking Assistance Systems refer to technologies that aid drivers in parking their vehicles safely and accurately. These systems can be categorized into different types. Reversing Radar relies on sensors located around the vehicle to detect obstacles and provide audio or visual warnings to the driver. Reverse Imaging utilizes cameras to display the rear view of the vehicle, allowing the driver to better judge their parking position. Panoramic Vision Parking Assist utilizes multiple cameras to create a virtual 360-degree view of the vehicle, aiding in parking manoeuvres. Other types of parking assistance systems include automated parking systems and parking sensors.</p></p>
