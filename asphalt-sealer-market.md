<p><p>Asphalt sealer is a protective layer applied on asphalt surfaces to extend their lifespan and enhance their appearance. The market for asphalt sealer is divided into two types: oil-based sealer and water-based sealer. Oil-based sealer is made from crude oil and provides excellent water resistance, flexibility, and durability. It is commonly used for high-traffic areas. On the other hand, water-based sealer is manufactured using water as a base, making it environmentally friendly. It is less durable but provides adequate protection for low-traffic areas. Both types have their distinct advantages and suitability based on the specific application.</p></p>
