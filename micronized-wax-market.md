There are different types of micronized wax available in the market. PE micronized wax is made from polyethylene and is commonly used in coatings, adhesives, and printing inks. PP micronized wax is derived from polypropylene and finds applications in plastics, rubber, and paints. PTFE micronized wax is based on polytetrafluoroethylene and is primarily used as a lubricant in industries such as automotive and aerospace. FT micronized wax is produced from Fischer-Tropsch synthesis and is often used in PVC processing. Additionally, there are other types of micronized waxes available to cater to specific needs in various industries.</p></p>
