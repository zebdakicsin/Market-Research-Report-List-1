It provides a comprehensive analysis of bone health and helps diagnose conditions like osteoporosis. On the other hand, Peripheral DEXA Bone Densitometer is a less complex and portable version, primarily used to measure bone density in the wrist, heel, or finger. It is a useful screening tool for initial evaluations.</p></p>
