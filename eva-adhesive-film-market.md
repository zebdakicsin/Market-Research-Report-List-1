<p><p>The EVA adhesive film market consists of two main types: transparent EVA film and white EVA film. Transparent EVA film is a clear adhesive film that is highly transparent and allows visibility through it. It is commonly used for applications where clarity and visibility are important, such as in packaging or laminating materials. On the other hand, white EVA film is an opaque adhesive film that offers a solid white appearance. It is often used for applications where an opaque and white surface is desired, such as in printing or graphic overlays.</p></p>
