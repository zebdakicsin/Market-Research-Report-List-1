<p><p>The Energy (UV/EB) Curable Resins Market is divided into two types: Ultraviolet (UV) Curing and Electron beam (EB) Curing. UV Curing involves the use of ultraviolet light to initiate a chemical reaction that hardens the resin. This method offers faster curing time and is commonly used in coatings, inks, and adhesives. EB Curing, on the other hand, utilizes high-energy electron beams to initiate the curing process. This method provides excellent depth of cure and is mostly used in applications that require thicker coatings or heat-sensitive materials.</p></p>
