<p><p>High Concentration Photovoltaics (HCPV) is a type of photovoltaic technology that utilizes lenses or mirrors to concentrate sunlight onto small, highly efficient solar cells. The concentrated sunlight intensifies the energy delivered to the cells, resulting in higher electricity generation compared to traditional solar panels. HCPV systems require precise tracking systems to ensure that the concentrators are always pointed directly at the sun.</p><p>One of the key advantages of HCPV is its ability to produce electricity at a significantly higher efficiency compared to conventional solar technologies. With concentrated sunlight, HCPV systems can achieve conversion efficiencies of over 40%, which is considerably higher than the typical efficiency range of 15-20% for other photovoltaic technologies. This high efficiency makes HCPV an attractive option for applications where space is limited, such as rooftop installations or solar farms.</p><p>The HCPV market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years.
