<p><p>The garage door operators market consists of various types of openers, including chain driven, screw driven, and belt driven options. Chain driven garage door openers are the most common and affordable, utilizing a metal chain to lift or lower the door. Screw driven openers use a threaded steel rod to move the door, providing a quieter operation than chain driven models. Belt driven openers use a rubber belt for smooth and virtually silent door movement. These different types offer customers a range of options based on their preferences and budget.</p></p>
