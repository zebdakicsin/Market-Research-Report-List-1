<p><p>The copper products market consists of various types of copper materials, including copper plates, copper strips, copper foils, copper tubes, copper rods, copper wires, and copper profiles. Copper plates are large flat sheets of copper used in industries like construction and electrical wiring. Copper strips are thin, elongated copper pieces used in conductive applications. Copper foils are even thinner and more flexible, mainly used in electronics. Copper tubes are hollow cylindrical pipes used for plumbing and HVAC systems. Copper rods are solid cylindrical bars used in construction and manufacturing. Copper wires are conductive strands used for electrical purposes. Copper profiles are specially shaped copper sections used in various industries.</p></p>
