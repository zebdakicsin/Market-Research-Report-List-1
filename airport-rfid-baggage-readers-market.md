<p><p>The Airport RFID Baggage Readers market is divided into two types: fixed type and portable type. The fixed type refers to RFID baggage readers that are permanently installed at specific locations within airports. They are generally used in areas where baggage needs to be automatically tracked, such as check-in counters or baggage sorting areas. On the other hand, portable type RFID baggage readers are designed to be carried by airport personnel or attached to movable equipment. They provide flexibility in reading baggage tags at various locations within the airport.</p></p>
