<p><p>Resin Black Matrix (RBM) is a material used in the manufacturing of Liquid Crystal Displays (LCDs). It is a black matrix that provides contrast to the pixels and improves the quality of the display. RBM is composed of a combination of resins and pigments, which offer superior optical properties such as excellent light blocking and high blackness.</p><p>The market analysis indicates that the Resin Black Matrix (RBM) market is poised for significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for high-quality displays in multiple industries, such as consumer electronics, automotive, and healthcare, is the primary driving factor for this market. The growth of the consumer electronics industry, especially smartphones and tablets, has created a surge in demand for RBM.</p><p>Another trend observed in the RBM market is the increasing replacement of Cathode Ray Tube (CRT) displays with LCDs. CRT displays are bulky, energy-consuming, and offer lower resolution compared to LCDs. As a result, there is a growing need for RBM in LCD production, further propelling the market growth.</p><p>The market is also witnessing a rise in the adoption of In-Plane Switching (IPS) technology, which offers enhanced color accuracy and wider viewing angles. This trend is expected to fuel the demand for RBM, as it is an essential component in IPS-LCD manufacturing.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing popularity of virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) technologies is expected to drive the RBM market.
