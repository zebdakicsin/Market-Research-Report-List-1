<p><p>The PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) Sputtering Targets market encompasses two main types: Metal Sputtering Targets and Compound Sputtering Targets. Metal Sputtering Targets refer to the use of metallic materials, such as gold or platinum, which are directly sputtered to provide a thin metallic coating. On the other hand, Compound Sputtering Targets involve the sputtering of compound materials, such as oxides or nitrides, which are used to create thin films with specific properties like corrosion resistance or optical transparency. These different types cater to diverse applications and industries.</p></p>
