<p><strong>The Distributed Raman Optical Amplifiers (DRA) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-Raman</li><li>Post Raman</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distributed Raman Optical Amplifiers (DRA) are used to enhance the transmission capacity of optical communication systems. In the pre-Raman market, Raman amplification technology is deployed before the transmission, where the signal is amplified prior to dispersion. This enables efficient signal transmission over long distances by compensating for power losses. In the post-Raman market, the Raman amplification technique is applied after the transmission, compensating for losses that occur during signal propagation. Both pre-Raman and post-Raman solutions have their merits, providing options for optimizing optical communication systems based on specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
