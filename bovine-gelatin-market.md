<p><p>Bovine gelatin is a type of gelatin extracted from the bones, hides, and connective tissues of cattle. Gelatin is a protein-rich substance that is commonly used in various industries, including the food and beverage, pharmaceutical, and cosmetic sectors. The rising demand for functional and processed food products, the growing popularity of convenience foods, and the increasing awareness about the health benefits of gelatin consumption are some of the key factors driving market growth. Bovine gelatin's excellent binding and gelling properties, as well as its ability to improve texture and extend shelf life, make it a highly sought-after ingredient in the food industry.</p><p>Additionally, the pharmaceutical and nutraceutical sectors are witnessing significant growth, further bolstering the demand for bovine gelatin. Gelatin capsules are widely used for drug delivery due to their ease of swallowing and rapid dissolution properties.
