<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The electromechanical solenoid valves market includes three main types: direct-acting solenoid valves, sub-step direct-acting solenoid valves, and pilot solenoid valves. Direct-acting solenoid valves operate by directly opening or closing the valve port using an electrical signal. Sub-step direct-acting solenoid valves have finer control capabilities as they can operate in small steps. Pilot solenoid valves control the flow of a larger valve using a smaller solenoid valve. These types of valves are commonly used in various industries for controlling the flow of liquids or gases.</p></p>
