<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Dual Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Screen printing machines for solar cell market types can be classified into single channel and dual channel. </p><p>A single channel machine has only one working station, allowing for printing on a single side of the solar cell at a time. This type of machine is suitable for smaller operations with lower production volumes.</p><p>On the other hand, a dual channel machine has two working stations, enabling printing on both sides of the solar cell simultaneously. This type of machine offers higher efficiency and productivity, making it the preferred choice for larger scale solar cell manufacturing.</p></p>
