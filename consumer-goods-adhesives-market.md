<p><strong>The Consumer Goods Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-component Adhesive</li><li>Two-component Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Consumer Goods Adhesives market types include one-component adhesive and two-component adhesive. One-component adhesives are pre-mixed and ready-to-use, eliminating the need for additional components. They offer convenience and ease of application but may have slower curing times. On the other hand, two-component adhesives require the activation of two separate components before application. They often have faster curing times and offer superior bond strength. However, they may require more complex handling and mixing processes. Both types play a crucial role in various consumer goods industries, such as automotive, electronics, and packaging.</p></p>
