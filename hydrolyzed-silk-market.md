<p><p>The hydrolyzed silk market consists of three main types: silk fibroin powder, silk amino acids, and silk peptide. Silk fibroin powder is a finely ground form of silk protein, known for its moisturizing and rejuvenating properties in skincare products. Silk amino acids are derived from silk fibroin and are used in hair and skincare products for their conditioning and strengthening effects. Silk peptide is a type of hydrolyzed silk that has smaller molecular weight, allowing for deeper penetration into the skin, making it popular in anti-aging skincare products.</p></p>
