<p><p>The WIFI and LTE coexistence filter market includes two main types of filters: band pass filters and band stop filters. Band pass filters allow specific frequencies to pass through while blocking others, enabling wifi and LTE signals to coexist without interference. On the other hand, band stop filters, also known as band reject filters, do the opposite. They reject specific frequencies, preventing them from interfering with wifi and LTE signals. Both types of filters play a crucial role in maintaining efficient and interference-free communication in wireless networks.</p></p>
