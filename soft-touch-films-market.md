<p><p>The soft touch films market can be categorized into three types: water-based, solvent-based, and FDA compliant soft touch coatings. Water-based soft touch films are environmentally friendly and provide a smooth texture. Solvent-based films offer enhanced durability and resistance to chemicals. FDA compliant soft touch coatings are specifically formulated to meet the regulations set by the Food and Drug Administration for applications that require direct contact with food or pharmaceutical products. These coatings provide a soft and tactile feel while ensuring the safety and compliance of the final product.</p></p>
