<p><p>The Bus HVAC System market can be categorized into three types: ICE Powered, Electric, and Hybrid. ICE Powered systems are equipped with an Internal Combustion Engine, which runs on fossil fuels such as diesel or gasoline, to power the HVAC unit. Electric systems utilize electric power sourced from batteries or overhead power lines. Hybrid systems combine both Internal Combustion Engines and electric power, providing greater flexibility and efficiency in terms of energy consumption and environmental impact. Each type offers different advantages and serves specific needs in the market.</p></p>
