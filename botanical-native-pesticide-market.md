<p><strong>The Botanical Native Pesticide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phytotoxin</li><li>Phytogenous Insect Hormone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Botanical Native Pesticide market consists of two main types: Phytotoxins and Phytogenous Insect Hormones. Phytotoxins are naturally occurring compounds produced by plants that have pesticidal properties. They can inhibit the growth and development of other plants, acting as natural herbicides. On the other hand, Phytogenous Insect Hormones are chemicals derived from plants that disrupt the normal development and behavior of insects. These hormones can affect insect growth, molting, reproduction, and even feeding patterns. Both types of pesticides offer natural alternatives to synthetic pesticides in agricultural and pest control practices.</p></p>
