<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Automotive Radar Transceiver market can be divided into two main types: BiCMOS and RFCMOS. BiCMOS stands for Bipolar Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor, which combines the benefits of both bipolar and CMOS technologies. This type offers high-speed and low-power advantages, making it suitable for radar applications in automobiles. On the other hand, RFCMOS, or Radio Frequency CMOS, integrates RF functionality with CMOS technology, providing a cost-effective solution for automotive radar systems. Both BiCMOS and RFCMOS technologies play a crucial role in enabling radar-based features in modern vehicles, ensuring improved safety and autonomous driving capabilities.</p></p>
