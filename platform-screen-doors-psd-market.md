<p><p>Platform screen doors (PSDs) are safety barriers installed at train or subway platforms to prevent accidents. They come in various market types. Full-closed type PSDs completely enclose the platform, providing maximum safety and preventing unauthorized access. Semi-closed type PSDs only enclose a portion of the platform, allowing some open-air space. Half-height type PSDs are waist-level barriers that create a physical separation between passengers and the tracks, while still allowing some ventilation. These different types cater to diverse requirements and preferences, ensuring safety while considering factors like climate, cost, and aesthetics.</p></p>
