<p><p>The hand-held slit lamps market includes two types: monocular hand-held slit lamps and binocular hand-held slit lamps. Monocular hand-held slit lamps are equipped with a single eyepiece, providing a one-eyed view of the patient's eye. Binocular hand-held slit lamps have two eyepieces, offering a stereoscopic view for enhanced depth perception. Both types are used by ophthalmologists to examine the eye's anterior segment and diagnose various eye conditions. These portable devices provide convenience and flexibility for eye examinations in diverse settings.</p></p>
