<p><p>The coating and inks additives market consists of various types of additives that serve different purposes. Rheology modifiers are used to control the flow and viscosity of the coating or ink. Dispersants help to evenly distribute the pigment particles. Foam control products reduce the formation of foam during application. Slip and rub materials enhance the smoothness and rub resistance of the coating. Wetting agents improve the adhesion of the coating to the substrate. Other additives may include antioxidants, UV stabilizers, and biocides, among others, which provide additional functionalities to the coating or ink.</p></p>
