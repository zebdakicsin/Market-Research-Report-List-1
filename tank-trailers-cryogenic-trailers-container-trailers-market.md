<p><p>Tank trailers, also known as cryogenic trailers and container trailers, are specialized vehicles designed for transporting liquids, gases, and bulk materials. These trailers are primarily used in various industries such as chemicals, oil and gas, food and beverages, and agriculture to transport commodities safely and efficiently.</p><p>Cryogenic trailers are specifically designed to transport liquefied gases such as nitrogen, oxygen, argon, and LNG at extremely low temperatures. They are heavily insulated to prevent the gases from vaporizing during transportation.</p><p>Container trailers, on the other hand, are used to transport bulk materials in intermodal containers. These trailers are equipped with a chassis to carry the container securely, providing easy loading and unloading at different locations.</p><p>The tank trailers market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. Several factors contribute to this growth, including increasing demand for transportation of liquefied gases, growth in the oil and gas industry, and rising global trade of chemicals.
