<p><p>The sugarless sweetener market encompasses two main types: natural and synthetic. On the other hand, synthetic sweeteners are chemically produced and can be several hundred times sweeter than sugar. These sweeteners include aspartame, saccharin, and sucralose. Both natural and synthetic sweeteners offer alternatives to sugar for those seeking reduced sugar or calorie intake in their diet.</p></p>
