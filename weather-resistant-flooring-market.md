<p><p>Weather resistant flooring is a type of flooring designed to withstand adverse weather conditions and elements. Three-layer weather resistant flooring consists of three layers - a top wear layer for durability, a middle layer for stability, and a bottom layer for moisture resistance. This type of flooring is often used in outdoor settings. Multilayer weather resistant flooring, on the other hand, consists of more than three layers. These additional layers may provide enhanced strength, flexibility, or insulation properties, making it suitable for various weather conditions and indoor/outdoor applications.</p></p>
