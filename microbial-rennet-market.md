The growing trend of vegetarianism and veganism, coupled with an increasing awareness of animal welfare issues, is driving the demand for plant-based and animal-free alternatives in the food industry. This has created a favorable market environment for microbial rennet as it caters to these consumer preferences.</p><p>In addition to its vegetarian and vegan appeal, microbial rennet offers several advantages over animal rennet, including higher enzyme activity, consistent quality, and reduced production costs.
