<p><p>Automated Truck Loading System (ATLS) is a technology that enables the automated loading and unloading of trucks or trailers in a warehouse or distribution center. There are different market types for ATLS, including the Flush Dock, Enclosed Dock, Saw Tooth Dock, and Others. In a Flush Dock layout, trucks are loaded or unloaded on a platform that is level with the warehouse floor. An Enclosed Dock has an enclosed loading area, providing protection from outside weather and additional security. The Saw Tooth Dock features loading bays arranged in a slope-like pattern, allowing trucks to back in at an angle. Other market types may include variations or combinations of these layouts.</p></p>
