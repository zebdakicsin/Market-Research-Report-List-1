<p><p>The titanium powder market is classified into two types: High Purity Titanium Powder (CPTP) and Alloyed Titanium Powder (ATP). CPTP refers to titanium powder with a high level of purity, typically above 99.5%. It is used in various industries such as aerospace, medical, and electronics due to its excellent corrosion resistance and high strength. On the other hand, ATP is titanium powder alloyed with other elements like aluminum or nickel. ATP finds applications in industries like automotive, defense, and power generation, offering improved mechanical properties and enhanced performance.</p></p>
