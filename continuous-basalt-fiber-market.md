<p><p>Continuous Basalt Fiber (CBF) is a type of material made from natural volcanic basalt rocks. These fibers are then coated with a sizing agent to improve their adhesion with other materials.</p><p>The properties of Continuous Basalt Fiber make it a desirable material in various industries. It has high strength, excellent resistance to heat and chemicals, good insulating properties, and low water absorption. CBF is widely used in the construction industry for reinforcing concrete structures, as it improves the durability and strength of the concrete. It is also used in the automotive, aerospace, and electrical industries for manufacturing lightweight and high-strength components. </p><p>The continuous basalt fiber market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period.
