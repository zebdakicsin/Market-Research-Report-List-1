<p><p>The polyurethane foam additives market consists of two main types: hard and soft. Hard foam additives are used to formulate rigid polyurethane foams that are typically used for insulation in buildings and appliances. These additives help improve thermal insulation properties and increase the overall strength and durability of the foam. On the other hand, soft foam additives are used to produce flexible polyurethane foams, which are widely used in furniture, bedding, and automotive applications for their cushioning and comfort properties. These additives enhance the foam's softness, flexibility, and resilience.</p></p>
