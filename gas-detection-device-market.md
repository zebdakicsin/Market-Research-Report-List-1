<p><strong>The Gas Detection Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Detector</li><li>Transmitter</li><li>Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas detection device market includes three main types of devices: detectors, transmitters, and controllers. Detectors are used to sense and detect the presence of hazardous gases, such as carbon monoxide or methane, in the environment. Transmitters are responsible for transmitting the detected gas data to a centralized control system or a remote display panel for monitoring and analysis. Controllers, on the other hand, are used to manage and control the entire gas detection system, including alarms, notifications, and calibration settings. These devices work together to ensure safety and mitigate the risks associated with gas leaks.</p></p>
