<p><p>The Liquid Crystalline Polyesters Market can be categorized into three main types: Injection Molding, Extrusion Molding, and Other markets. Injection molding involves injecting the liquid crystalline polyester into a mold cavity, allowing it to cool and solidify into the desired shape. Extrusion molding, on the other hand, involves pushing the material through a die to create a continuous profile with a specific cross-sectional shape.
