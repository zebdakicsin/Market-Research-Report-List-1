<p><strong>The Semiconductor Glass Substrates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alkali Glass</li><li>Alkali-free Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor glass substrates are widely used in the manufacturing of electronic components, such as integrated circuits and display screens. There are two main types of semiconductor glass substrates in the market - alkali glass and alkali-free glass. </p><p>Alkali glass substrates contain alkali metals, which provide better electrical conductivity but are more susceptible to contamination. On the other hand, alkali-free glass substrates do not contain alkali metals, making them more resistant to contamination but with slightly lower electrical conductivity. The choice of substrate depends on the specific needs of the semiconductor application, balancing conductivity and contamination resistance.</p></p>
