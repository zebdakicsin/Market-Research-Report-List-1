<p><p>The vehicle-mounted LED display market consists of three types: single base color, double base color, and full color. A single base color LED display utilizes only one base color, such as red or green, to emit light. A double base color LED display combines two base colors to produce a wider range of colors, providing more flexibility in displaying content. On the other hand, a full color LED display offers a complete spectrum of colors, allowing for high-resolution and vibrant visuals. These different types cater to varying customer requirements and preferences in terms of display quality and cost.</p></p>
