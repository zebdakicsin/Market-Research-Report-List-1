<p><p>Stretch hood films are used for secure packaging of products on pallets. The market types of stretch hood films include LDPE (low-density polyethylene), HDPE (high-density polyethylene), LLDPE (linear low-density polyethylene), Ethylene Vinyl Acetate, Plastomers & Elastomers, and Others. LDPE films are flexible and have good tear resistance, while HDPE films are stiffer and provide better load stability. LLDPE films have a combination of properties from both LDPE and HDPE. Ethylene Vinyl Acetate films offer good elasticity and transparency. Plastomers & elastomers are high-performance films with exceptional strength and elasticity. The "Others" category includes films made from different materials or blends.</p></p>
