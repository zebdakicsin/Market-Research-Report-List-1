<p><p>Precast concrete release agents are substances used to prevent concrete from bonding to the surface of molds or forms during the precasting process. There are two types of release agents: oil-based and water-based. Oil-based release agents are made from petroleum-based materials and offer excellent lubrication properties, resulting in smooth and easy release of the concrete from the molds. Water-based release agents, on the other hand, are typically made from natural or synthetic ingredients diluted with water. They provide a more environmentally friendly option but may require more frequent application compared to oil-based agents.</p></p>
