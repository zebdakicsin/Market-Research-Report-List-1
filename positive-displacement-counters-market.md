<p><p>Positive displacement counters are devices used to measure the flow rate or volume of liquids or gases in various industrial applications. They operate by trapping a fixed volume of fluid within a measuring chamber and then repeatedly filling and emptying the chamber, keeping count of the number of times this volume is displaced. This count is then used to calculate the flow rate or total volume of the fluid being measured.</p><p>Market analysis suggests that the positive displacement counters market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
