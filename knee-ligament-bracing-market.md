<p><strong>The Knee Ligament Bracing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Support</li><li>External Support</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The knee ligament bracing market consists of two main types: internal support and external support. Internal support braces are worn inside the knee and provide stability by restricting the movement of the ligaments. External support braces, on the other hand, are applied externally around the knee to offer compression and stabilize the ligaments from outside. Both types aim to prevent further injury or strain to the knee ligaments and provide support during physical activity or recovery.</p></p>
