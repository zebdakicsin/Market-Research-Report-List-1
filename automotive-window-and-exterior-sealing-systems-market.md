<p><p>Automotive window and exterior sealing systems refer to various types of seals used in vehicles to prevent water, wind, dust, and noise from entering the cabin. The three main market types in this industry are EPDM sealing systems, TPE/TPO sealing systems, and PVC sealing systems. EPDM sealing systems are made from a synthetic rubber material known for its durability and resistance to weathering. TPE/TPO sealing systems use thermoplastic elastomers to provide flexibility and excellent sealing properties. PVC sealing systems are made from polyvinyl chloride and offer good resistance to chemicals and abrasion.</p></p>
