<p><p>The Eye-Safe Laser Range Finder market includes three major types: Flashlamp Pumped, Diode Pumped, and Others. Diode Pumped range finders utilize diode-pumped solid-state lasers, enabling higher repetition rates and reduced size. The Others category comprises alternative technologies like fiber lasers or hybrid systems that offer unique advantages.
