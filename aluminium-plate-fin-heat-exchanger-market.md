<p><strong>The Aluminium Plate-fin Heat Exchanger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Aluminium plate-fin heat exchangers are classified into different types based on the design of the fins. </p><p>1. PlaneFin: This type features straight and smooth fins, providing a large surface area for heat transfer. It is commonly used in various industries for efficient cooling and heating applications.</p><p>2. SerratedFin: Serrated fins have a zigzag pattern, which enhances turbulence and increases heat transfer efficiency. This type is often used in applications where high performance is required, such as aerospace and automotive industries.</p><p>3. PorousFin: Porous fins are made with interconnected pores that promote fluid flow and enhance heat transfer. They are suitable for applications that involve high-pressure and high-velocity fluids, offering improved performance and lower pressure drop.</p><p>4. Other market: This category comprises various niche types of aluminium plate-fin heat exchangers that are designed for specific applications or unique requirements. These may include offset fin designs, multi-louvered fins, or customized configurations tailored for specific industries or operating conditions.</p></p>
