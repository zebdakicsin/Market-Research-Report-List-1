<p><ul><li>Single ply products</li><li>Modified bitumen materials</li><li>Spray polyurethane foam</li><li>Built-up roofing</li><li>Metals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial roofing materials market types include single ply products, which are flexible membranes made from materials like EPDM, PVC, or TPO that are easy to install and maintain. Modified bitumen materials are asphalt-based sheets reinforced with polyester or fiberglass for enhanced durability and flexibility. Spray polyurethane foam is a liquid that expands into a seamless insulation layer and provides excellent thermal resistance. Built-up roofing consists of multiple layers of bitumen and reinforced fabrics. Metals like steel or aluminum are also used as commercial roofing materials due to their strength, durability, and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
