<p><strong>The Carbon / Sulfur Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tube Type</li><li>Arc Type</li><li>High Frequency Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Carbon/Sulfur Analyzer market can be classified into three types: Tube Type, Arc Type, and High Frequency Type. Tube Type analyzers measure carbon and sulfur by extracting and purifying gases generated from a sample. Arc Type analyzers use high temperatures to combust a sample and measure the released carbon and sulfur oxide gases. High Frequency Type analyzers utilize high-frequency induction furnace technology to determine carbon and sulfur content. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in various industries such as metallurgy, petrochemical, and environmental analysis.</p></p>
