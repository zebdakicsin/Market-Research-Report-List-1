<p><p>The air conditioner accessories market includes various types of components such as refrigerant, compressor, condenser coil, expansion valve, and evaporator coil. Refrigerant is the substance responsible for cooling and can be in the form of gas or liquid. Compressors pressurize and circulate the refrigerant, while condenser coils release the heat outside the AC unit. Expansion valves regulate the flow and pressure of the refrigerant. Evaporator coils absorb heat from the indoor air and facilitate cooling. These accessories are crucial for the efficient functioning of air conditioning systems.</p></p>
