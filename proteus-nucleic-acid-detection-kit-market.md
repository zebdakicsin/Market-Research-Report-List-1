<p><p>The Proteus Nucleic Acid Detection Kit is available in two market types, namely Dry PCR and Fluorescence PCR. The Dry PCR market refers to a type of nucleic acid detection wherein the polymerase chain reaction (PCR) process is performed in a dry format, eliminating the need for liquid reagents. This allows for easier handling and storage of the kit components. On the other hand, Fluorescence PCR market refers to a nucleic acid detection method that utilizes fluorescent dyes to amplify and detect specific nucleic acid targets. This method provides higher sensitivity and precision in detecting genetic materials.</p></p>
