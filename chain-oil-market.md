<p><p>The chain oil market is divided into three major types: mineral oil type, polyether type, and ester oil type. Mineral oil type chain oils are petroleum-based and offer good lubrication properties at a lower cost. Polyether type chain oils are synthetic oils that provide excellent wear protection and are resistant to high temperatures. Ester oil type chain oils are also synthetic oils and offer high lubricity and resistance to oxidation. These different types cater to varying needs in terms of cost, performance, and durability.</p></p>
