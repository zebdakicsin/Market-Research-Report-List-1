<p><p>Continuous track wheels, also known as caterpillar tracks, are a type of wheels commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles such as tanks and construction equipment. The growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for heavy-duty vehicles in various sectors such as construction, mining, and defense. These wheels provide enhanced stability, traction, and maneuverability, making them ideal for rough terrains. Additionally, continuous track wheels offer lower ground pressure, reducing the chances of damage to the ground surface and improving overall performance.</p><p>In recent years, there has been a trend towards the development of technologically advanced continuous track wheels. Manufacturers are focusing on improving durability, reducing weight, increasing load-carrying capacity, and enhancing fuel efficiency.
