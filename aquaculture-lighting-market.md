<p><p>Aquaculture Lighting Market includes two major types of lighting: Halogen Lighting and LED Lighting. Halogen Lighting consists of halogen bulbs that emit bright light using a heated filament, providing a warm and intense illumination. On the other hand, LED Lighting utilizes light-emitting diodes to generate efficient and long-lasting light. LED Lighting offers various advantages such as energy efficiency, customizable light spectrum, and low heat emission. Both types play a crucial role in aquaculture, providing sufficient light for the growth and development of aquatic organisms.</p></p>
