<p><p>Peristaltic pumps are commonly used in the municipal water and wastewater industry. There are two main types: roller peristaltic pumps and shoe type peristaltic pumps. Roller peristaltic pumps use rotating rollers to squeeze a hose or tube, creating a squeezing action that moves fluids. They are suitable for medium to high flow rates. Shoe type peristaltic pumps use a shoe or finger mechanism to compress the hose or tube, making them ideal for low flow rates and accurate dosing applications in the water and wastewater market.</p></p>
