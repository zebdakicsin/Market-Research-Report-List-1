<p><strong>The Emulsion Breaker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil Soluble Demulsifying Agent</li><li>Water Soluble Demulsifying Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Emulsion Breaker market consists of two main types: Oil Soluble Demulsifying Agents and Water Soluble Demulsifying Agents. Oil Soluble Demulsifying Agents are substances that are able to separate oil from water-based emulsions by bonding with the oil droplets and causing them to coalesce, forming larger droplets that can be easily separated. Water Soluble Demulsifying Agents, on the other hand, are substances that dissolve in water and help to break down the emulsion by destabilizing the oil-water interface. Both types of demulsifying agents play important roles in various industries where the separation of oil from water is crucial.</p></p>
