Circular saw blades are designed for making straight cuts in wood, metal, or other materials. Reciprocating saw blades have a back-and-forth cutting action, ideal for demolition work or cutting through materials like PVC or metal pipes. Jigsaw blades have a thin, narrow design and are perfect for making intricate curved or detailed cuts in wood or metal. Each type of saw blade caters to specific cutting needs in various industries and DIY projects.</p></p>
