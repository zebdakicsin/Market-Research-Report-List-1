<p><p>Flat roof waterproofing systems are used to protect buildings from water seepage through the roof. There are several types of systems available in the market. Waterproof membranes are thin sheets made of synthetic materials that are applied to the roof surface. Reinforced asphalt film is a combination of asphalt and polyester or fiberglass. Monolayer films are single-layered waterproofing systems. Liquid roofs consist of liquid membranes applied to the surface. Frankincense asphalt system uses a special type of asphalt reinforced with synthetic fibers. These systems help ensure a watertight roof surface.</p></p>
