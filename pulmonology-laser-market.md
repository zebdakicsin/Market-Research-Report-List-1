<p><p>The pulmonology laser market consists of different types of lasers used to treat lung-related conditions. One such type is the YAG laser, which utilizes yttrium-aluminum-garnet crystals to generate a laser beam mainly used for tissue ablation and coagulation. Another type is the photodynamic laser, which involves a photosensitizing agent that, upon activation by laser light, selectively destroys abnormal lung tissue. Both YAG and photodynamic lasers play a crucial role in improving patient outcomes by providing minimally invasive treatment options for various pulmonary disorders.</p></p>
