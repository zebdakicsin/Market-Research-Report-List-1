<p><strong>The Wind Turbine Composite Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Resin</li><li>Thermoplastic Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wind turbine composite materials market is segmented into two types based on the types of resins used - thermosetting resin and thermoplastic resin. Thermosetting resin refers to a type of polymer that, once cured, cannot be melted or reformed. This type of resin offers high strength and durability, making it suitable for wind turbine components exposed to harsh environmental conditions. On the other hand, thermoplastic resin can be melted and reformed multiple times without losing its properties. This type of resin offers excellent recyclability and provides ease of processing, making it a growing choice in the wind turbine industry.</p></p>
