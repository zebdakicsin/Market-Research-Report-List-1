<p><p>Electronically Commutated (EC) motor is a type of motor used in various applications. It can be categorized into two types: Inner Rotor Motor and Outer Rotor Motor. The Inner Rotor Motor has its rotor positioned inside the stator, resulting in a compact size and high torque output. On the other hand, the Outer Rotor Motor has its rotor positioned outside the stator, enabling better cooling and higher efficiency. Both types offer advantages depending on the specific application requirements, such as space limitations or cooling needs.</p></p>
