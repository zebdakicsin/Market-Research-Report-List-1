<p><p>Forklift gearboxes can be categorized into two main types: internal combustion gearbox and electric gearbox. The internal combustion gearbox is designed to support forklifts powered by internal combustion engines, such as gasoline or diesel. It efficiently transfers power from the engine to the wheels, enabling smooth movement and control. On the other hand, the electric gearbox is specifically developed for electric-powered forklifts. It performs a similar function as the internal combustion gearbox, efficiently transmitting power from the electric motor to the wheels, ensuring seamless operation and maneuverability.</p></p>
