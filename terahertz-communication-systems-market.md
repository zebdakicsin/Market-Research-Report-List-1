<p><p>Terahertz communication systems comprise three main market types: antennas, emitters, and modulators. Antennas are devices used to transmit and receive terahertz signals. They play a crucial role in establishing wireless connectivity. Emitters are responsible for generating terahertz waves, which are then transmitted via antennas. Modulators, on the other hand, control the characteristics and properties of these waves, allowing for efficient data transmission. All three components are essential to the functioning of terahertz communication systems, enabling high-speed wireless communication in various applications.</p></p>
