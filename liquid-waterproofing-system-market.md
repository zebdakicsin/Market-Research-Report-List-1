<p><p>The liquid waterproofing system market comprises various types such as PMMA, polyurethane, SEBS, and others. PMMA (polymethyl methacrylate) is known for its high durability and UV resistance, making it suitable for areas exposed to sunlight. Polyurethane offers excellent adhesion properties and is commonly used for roof waterproofing. SEBS (styrene ethylene butylene styrene) is a flexible and durable material, often used for balconies and terraces. The "others" category includes various materials like acrylic, silicone, and bitumen-based liquid waterproofing systems, each offering unique advantages for different waterproofing applications.</p></p>
