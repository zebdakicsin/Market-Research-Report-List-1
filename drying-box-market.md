<p><p>The drying box market includes various types, such as electrothermal drying boxes and vacuum drying boxes. Electrothermal drying boxes utilize electric heating elements to generate heat and dry materials. They are commonly used in laboratories and industrial settings due to their precise temperature control and uniform heat distribution. Vacuum drying boxes, on the other hand, create a low-pressure environment by removing air or gas, which accelerates the drying process. They are often used for heat-sensitive materials or substances that require a faster drying time. Both types of drying boxes cater to different needs and applications within the market.</p></p>
