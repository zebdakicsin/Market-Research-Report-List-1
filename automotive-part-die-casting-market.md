<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The automotive part die casting market includes various types of die casting processes. Pressure die casting involves injecting molten metal into a die under high pressure. Vacuum die casting uses a vacuum to help remove air from the die cavity during the casting process. Squeeze die casting applies additional pressure on the molten metal to compress it into the die cavity. Semi-solid die casting utilizes a partially solidified metal slurry and injects it into the die. These different processes offer manufacturers flexibility in producing automotive parts with different levels of complexity and structural integrity.</p></p>
