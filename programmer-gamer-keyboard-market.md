<p><p>The programmer/gamer keyboard market consists of two main types: mechanical keyboards and membrane keyboards. Mechanical keyboards are known for their durability and precise key actuation, as each key has its own mechanical switch. They provide a tactile and satisfying typing experience, preferred by many programmers and gamers. On the other hand, membrane keyboards use a silicone or rubber dome to register key presses, resulting in a quieter and more affordable option. While they may lack the same level of responsiveness as mechanical keyboards, membrane keyboards are still popular among casual users due to their affordability.</p></p>
