<p><p>The Immunoglobulin Products Market consists of various types of antibodies: IgA, IgG, IgM, IgD, and IgE. IgA is primarily found in body fluids and protects mucous membranes. IgG plays a crucial role in immune responses, providing long-term immunity and crossing the placenta to protect newborns. IgM is the first antibody produced during an infection and is responsible for early immune responses. IgD's exact function is unknown, but it is thought to aid in B-cell activation. IgE is related to allergic reactions and defends against parasites. These different types of immunoglobulins serve important roles in our immune system.</p></p>
