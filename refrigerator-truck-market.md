<p><p>The refrigerator truck market consists of two main types: single temperature type and multi-temperature type. A single temperature type refers to trucks that are designed to maintain a consistent temperature throughout the entire cargo space. These trucks are commonly used for transporting perishable goods that require a specific temperature range. On the other hand, multi-temperature type trucks have separate compartments with different temperature zones. This allows the transportation of various goods with different temperature requirements, providing flexibility and enabling efficient delivery of a wider range of products.</p></p>
