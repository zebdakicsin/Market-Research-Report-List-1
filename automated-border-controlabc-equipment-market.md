<p><p>The Automated Border Control (ABC) Equipment market comprises two main types: ABC e-gate and ABC kiosk. ABC e-gates are automated gates equipped with biometric sensors that verify travelers' identities through facial recognition or fingerprint scanning. These gates streamline the immigration process by expediting the entry or exit of individuals with pre-registered data. On the other hand, ABC kiosks are self-service machines that allow travelers to complete immigration processes independently, including filling out customs declarations and presenting proper documentation. Both types aim to enhance border security and improve the overall efficiency of border control procedures.</p></p>
