Straight barriers are fixed, rigid barriers that provide a permanent solution for access control. Folding barriers, on the other hand, are collapsible and can be easily set up or moved as per requirement. Other types of access control barriers include bollards, turnstiles, and gates, which offer additional functionalities such as pedestrian control or vehicle access management. These barriers play a crucial role in maintaining security and controlling the flow of people or vehicles in various environments.</p></p>
