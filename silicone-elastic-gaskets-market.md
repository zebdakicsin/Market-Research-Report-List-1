<p><strong>The Silicone Elastic Gaskets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Saddle</li><li>Waveform</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone elastic gaskets come in different types, including saddle and waveform gaskets. Saddle gaskets are shaped like a saddle and are designed to provide a tight seal between two surfaces. They are commonly used in plumbing and automotive applications. On the other hand, waveform gaskets have a wavy shape and are used to absorb vibration and compensate for irregularities between mating surfaces. They are often used in electrical equipment, machinery, and automotive exhaust systems. Both gasket types are made from silicone, a flexible and durable material that can withstand varying temperatures and resist moisture and chemicals.</p></p>
