<p><p>Enzymes play a crucial role in the textile industry. Different types of enzymes are utilized based on their specific functions. Cellulase enzymes break down cellulose fibers, enhancing fabric softness and providing a distressed or stone-washed appearance. Amylase enzymes remove starch-based sizing agents from textiles. Catalase enzymes help in removing residual hydrogen peroxide post-bleaching, preventing color fading. Pectinase enzymes assist in the removal of pectin-based impurities. Lastly, various other enzymes such as protease and lipase are used for fabric bio-polishing and stain removal. These enzymes significantly enhance textile quality and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
