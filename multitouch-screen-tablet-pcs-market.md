Resistive technology allows touch motion through pressure applied to two layers of conductive materials. Acoustic technology employs microphones and speakers to detect touch-related disturbances on the screen. Other market types may include infrared and optical touch technologies, each utilizing different approaches to detect touch and provide interactive experiences on tablet PCs.</p></p>
