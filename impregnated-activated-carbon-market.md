<p><p>The impregnated activated carbon market comprises two main types: coal-based activated carbon and coconut-based activated carbon. Coal-based activated carbon is derived from coal and has excellent adsorption properties due to its large surface area and high carbon content. It is widely used in applications such as gas purification, water treatment, and air filtration. On the other hand, coconut-based activated carbon is derived from coconut shells and is known for its high hardness, durability, and low ash content. It is commonly used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food processing, and cosmetics due to its superior adsorption capacity.</p></p>
