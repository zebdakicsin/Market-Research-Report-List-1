<p><ul><li>Air Horns</li><li>Electronic Horns</li><li>Electromagnetic Horns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive horns market consists of three main types: air horns, electronic horns, and electromagnetic horns. Air horns rely on a supply of compressed air to create a loud sound and are commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles. On the other hand, electronic horns generate sound through the use of an electronic circuit and are found in smaller vehicles. Lastly, electromagnetic horns produce sound by the vibration of a diaphragm and are typically used in motorcycles. These different types cater to various vehicle sizes and usage scenarios.</p></p>
