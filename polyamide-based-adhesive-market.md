<p><p>The polyamide-based adhesive market encompasses two types of adhesives: water-soluble adhesive and hot melt adhesive. Water-soluble adhesives are capable of dissolving in water and are commonly used in applications where easy clean-up is required. On the other hand, hot melt adhesives are solid at room temperature but melt when heated, allowing for bonding upon cooling. These adhesives are widely used in various industries due to their strong bonding capabilities and quick setting time.</p></p>
