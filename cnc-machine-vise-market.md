<p><p>The CNC machine vise market can be categorized into three types: single station, dual station, and multiple station markets. In the single station market, there is only one station available for holding the workpiece. In the dual station market, two stations are provided, allowing for simultaneous operation or set-up on two workpieces. Lastly, the multiple station market offers more than two stations, enabling operators to work on multiple workpieces simultaneously. These different market types cater to varying needs and requirements of manufacturers depending on their production capacity and efficiency goals.</p></p>
