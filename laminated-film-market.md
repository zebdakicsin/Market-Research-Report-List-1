<p><p>The laminated film market is divided into two main types: metal foil and thin paper. Metal foil laminated film refers to a type of packaging material that consists of a thin layer of metal foil which provides excellent barrier properties against moisture, oxygen, and light. This type of laminated film is commonly used for packaging of food products and pharmaceuticals. On the other hand, thin paper laminated film is a packaging material that combines a thin layer of paper with a layer of plastic film. This type of film offers good printability and is often used for packaging snacks, confectioneries, and personal care products.</p></p>
