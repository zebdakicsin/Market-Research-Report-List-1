Halogen reactive dyes contain halogen elements like chlorine or bromine in their structure, providing high color intensity and fastness. Activated Vinyl Compound reactive dyes are made from compounds with activated vinyl groups, resulting in vibrant colors and good light stability. The category of Others refers to any reactive dyes that do not fit into the specific Halogen or Activated Vinyl Compound classifications.</p></p>
