<p><p>Dry Eye Diagnosis Systems market includes various types of diagnostic devices used for the evaluation and diagnosis of dry eye disease. These include Anterior Segment OCT (AS-OCT), which provides high-resolution imaging of the eye's anterior segment. Ocular Surface Thermographer measures the temperature of the ocular surface to assess dryness. Corneal Topographer maps the shape of the cornea, aiding in dry eye diagnosis. Osmolarity Testing Device measures tear osmolarity levels. Fluorophotometer measures tear film break-up time. Interferometer measures tear film thickness. Meibography Device images the Meibomian glands. Other devices may also be used for diagnosis.</p></p>
