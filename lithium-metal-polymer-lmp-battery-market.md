<p><p>The Lithium-Metal-Polymer (LMP) battery market includes two types of batteries: Polymer-Based Lithium Solid-State Battery and Lithium Solid-State Battery with Inorganic Solid Electrolytes. </p><p>Polymer-Based Lithium Solid-State Batteries use polymer-based electrolytes to transfer lithium ions between the positive and negative electrodes. They offer high energy density, long cycle life, and improved safety compared to traditional lithium-ion batteries.</p><p>Lithium Solid-State Batteries with Inorganic Solid Electrolytes use inorganic solid electrolytes, such as ceramics, to enable the transfer of lithium ions. These batteries also offer high energy density and increased safety, while providing better stability and performance at higher temperatures.</p></p>
