<p><p>The disposable non-stick bipolar forceps market is categorized into four types: straight, angled, bayonet, and bent forceps. Straight forceps have a linear design, making them suitable for precise and direct tissue manipulation. Angled forceps feature a curved shaft at an angle of 45-90 degrees, allowing easy access to difficult-to-reach areas. Bayonet forceps have a slight curve at the tip, enhancing maneuverability during surgical procedures. Bent forceps have a pronounced bend towards the tip, enabling surgeons to access tight spaces or manipulate delicate tissues with precision.</p></p>
