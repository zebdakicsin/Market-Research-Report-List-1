<p><p>PV stringer machines are used in the solar industry for assembling photovoltaic modules. There are two main types of market for these machines: Mono-rail stringer machines and dual-rail stringer machines. Mono-rail stringer machines have a single rail system, allowing for a more compact design. They are suitable for smaller production lines and are often preferred for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Dual-rail stringer machines, on the other hand, have two parallel rail systems, enabling higher production capacities. They are commonly used in larger-scale manufacturing facilities to increase efficiency and output.</p></p>
