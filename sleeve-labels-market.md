<p><p>Sleeve labels are used in packaging to enhance the appearance of a product and provide vital information. Stretch sleeves are made from highly flexible material that can stretch and conform to the shape of a container, providing a seamless 360-degree branding area. On the other hand, shrink sleeves are made from heat-sensitive material that shrinks tightly onto the container when heat is applied, offering a tamper-evident seal. Both types of sleeve labels offer aesthetically pleasing designs and excellent durability while serving different packaging needs.</p></p>
