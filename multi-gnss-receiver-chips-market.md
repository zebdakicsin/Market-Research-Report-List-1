<p><p>Multi GNSS (Global Navigation Satellite System) receiver chips are used in various applications like automotive, aviation, and consumer electronics to accurately determine the position, velocity, and time. Single frequency receiver chips receive signals from only one satellite frequency and are affordable options suitable for basic navigation requirements. On the other hand, dual frequency receiver chips can receive signals from two different satellite frequencies, providing enhanced accuracy and performance, especially in challenging environments. These chips are typically preferred in demanding applications like high-precision positioning, surveying, and geodetic measurements.</p></p>
