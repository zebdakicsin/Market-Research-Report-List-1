<p><p>The autoclave indicator tape market consists of different types including steam indicator tapes, hydrogen peroxide tapes, EO sterilization indicator tapes, and dry heat tapes. Steam indicator tapes are used to indicate when an item has been exposed to steam sterilization. Hydrogen peroxide tapes serve the same purpose but for hydrogen peroxide sterilization. EO sterilization indicator tapes are designed for ethylene oxide sterilization, while dry heat tapes indicate exposure to dry heat sterilization. These tapes are essential tools in healthcare settings to ensure proper sterilization processes and prevent the spread of infections.</p></p>
