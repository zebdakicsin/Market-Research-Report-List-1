<p><p>The hot-pressed magnet market consists of two main types: hot-pressed isotropic neodymium iron boron (NdFeB) magnets and hot extrusion anisotropic NdFeB magnets. Hot-pressed isotropic NdFeB magnets are made using a powder metallurgy process and exhibit equal magnetic properties in all directions. On the other hand, hot extrusion anisotropic NdFeB magnets are manufactured through a technique called hot extrusion, resulting in magnets with different magnetic properties in different directions. Both types of magnets are used in various industries for their strong magnetic properties and durability.</p></p>
