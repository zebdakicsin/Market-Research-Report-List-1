<p><p>Sapphire crystal is available in two market types, namely artificial and natural sapphire crystal. Artificial sapphire crystal is produced in the laboratory using advanced technologies, mimicking the chemical composition of natural sapphire. It provides a cost-effective solution and is widely used in various applications like electronics and optics. On the other hand, natural sapphire crystal is formed under natural geological processes within the Earth's crust. Due to its rarity and unique beauty, it finds applications in high-end jewelry and luxury watches. Both market types cater to different needs and preferences in different industries.</p></p>
