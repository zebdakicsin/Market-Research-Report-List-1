<p><p>Spring couplings are used to transmit torque and accommodate misalignment between two rotating shafts. The market for spring couplings can be categorized into three types: coil spring coupling, serpentine spring coupling, and others. A coil spring coupling consists of helical springs that provide flexibility and allow for a high torque capacity. Serpentine spring couplings utilize flat springs that can handle lower torque levels but offer better misalignment tolerance. Lastly, the "others" category includes various spring coupling designs, such as disc spring couplings and bellows couplings, that cater to specific applications and requirements.</p></p>
