<p><p>The flexible rubber sheeting market comprises various types, such as NBR (nitrile butadiene rubber) based, EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) based, chloroprene based, and others. NBR based sheets offer excellent resistance to oils, fuels, and chemicals. EPDM based sheets exhibit exceptional weather and ozone resistance, making them suitable for outdoor applications. Chloroprene based sheets possess good flame retardant properties. The "others" category includes various rubber sheeting types like natural rubber, silicone rubber, and butyl rubber, each offering unique characteristics for specific applications.</p></p>
