<p><p>The Electronic Brake System (EBS) market comprises two main types: automatic and semi-automatic systems. Automatic systems use sensors and electronic control units to monitor braking conditions and automatically adjust brake pressure accordingly, providing enhanced stability and control during braking. Semi-automatic systems, on the other hand, require the driver to manually control the brake pressure but still benefit from electronic assistance and monitoring. Both types aim to improve braking performance and safety by incorporating electronic technology into traditional brake systems.</p></p>
