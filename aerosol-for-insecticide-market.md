<p><p>The aerosol insecticide market is segmented into two main types: aerosols and triggers. Aerosols refer to pressurized containers that release a fine mist or spray when activated. They are convenient and easy to use, providing a wide coverage area. On the other hand, triggers are non-pressurized containers equipped with a pump mechanism that releases the insecticide when the trigger is squeezed. Triggers are often used for smaller areas or targeted applications, as they offer more control over the amount and direction of the spray.</p></p>
