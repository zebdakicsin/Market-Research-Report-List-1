<p><p>The automotive side glass market includes three main types: Class A Laminated Glass, Class B Laminated Glass, and Tempered Glass. Class A Laminated Glass is made of two layers of glass with an interlayer in between, providing enhanced safety and protection. Class B Laminated Glass also consists of two layers with an interlayer, but offers reduced thickness compared to Class A. Lastly, Tempered Glass undergoes a controlled heating and cooling process to increase its strength and safety. These different types cater to various automotive safety requirements and preferences.</p></p>
