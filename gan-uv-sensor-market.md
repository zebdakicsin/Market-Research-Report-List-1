<p><p>The GaN UV Sensor market includes three main types of sensors: UVA Sensor, UVB Sensor, and UVC Sensor. UVA Sensors are designed to detect UVA rays, which have longer wavelengths and can penetrate deeper into the skin, potentially causing long-term effects. UVB Sensors focus on detecting UVB rays, which have shorter wavelengths and mainly cause sunburns and skin damage. Lastly, UVC Sensors are engineered to detect UVC rays, which have the shortest wavelengths and are typically absorbed by the Earth's atmosphere but can be harmful if exposed to humans.</p></p>
