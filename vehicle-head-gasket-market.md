<p><p>The vehicle head gasket market is divided into three types: non-metallic, semi-metallic, and metallic. Non-metallic gaskets are made from materials like rubber or graphite, which provide a high level of sealing but may not withstand high temperatures. Semi-metallic gaskets combine materials like graphite and metal, offering better resilience to temperature and pressure fluctuations. Metallic gaskets are made entirely of metal, providing excellent durability and sealing performance under extreme conditions. Each market type caters to different needs and requirements in terms of sealing capabilities, temperature resistance, and longevity in vehicle engines.</p></p>
