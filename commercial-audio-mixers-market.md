Wireless Connectivity: Commercial audio mixers with wireless connectivity options are becoming increasingly popular, eliminating the need for complex wired connections and offering more flexibility in setup and operation.</p><p>2. Integration of Digital Signal Processing (DSP): Many commercial audio mixers now incorporate DSP features, allowing sound engineers to enhance audio quality, apply effects, and adjust the dynamics of audio signals in real-time.</p><p>3.
