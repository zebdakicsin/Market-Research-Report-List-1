<p><ul><li>Batch</li><li>Continuous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial frying system market is divided into two main types: batch and continuous. In batch frying, food products are fried in small, separate batches. This process is ideal for smaller production volumes and allows for more control over frying time and oil temperature. On the other hand, continuous frying involves a continuous flow of food products through the frying system. This method is suitable for high-volume production and offers higher efficiency and productivity due to its continuous operation.</p></p>
