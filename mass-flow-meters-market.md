<p><p>Mass flow meters are devices used to measure the flow rate of fluids, such as gases and liquids, in various industrial applications. Gas mass flow meters are designed to precisely measure the flow rate of gases based on thermal, pressure, or Coriolis principles. Liquid mass flow meters, on the other hand, are used to measure the flow rate of liquids, with types including electromagnetic, ultrasonic, or positive displacement meters. Both types ensure accurate and reliable measurement of fluid flow, enabling efficient control and monitoring of various processes.</p></p>
