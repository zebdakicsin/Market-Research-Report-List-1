<p><p>The X-Ray Films Market comprises different types of films used for capturing X-ray images. Screen type films are designed to be used with intensifying screens, which enhance the images when exposed to X-rays. Non-screen type films, on the other hand, do not require the use of intensifying screens and produce images directly with the help of specialized X-ray machines. The "Others" market category includes alternative methods and technologies for capturing X-ray images, such as digital imaging systems or computed radiography.</p></p>
