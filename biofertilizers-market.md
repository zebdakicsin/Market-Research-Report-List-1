<p><p>The biofertilizers market comprises various types of beneficial microorganisms that facilitate plant growth. Azotobacter, Azospirillium, Rhizobium, Phosphate-Solubilizing Bacteria, and Cyanobacteria are key types. Azotobacter enhances nitrogen fixation and nutrient uptake, while Azospirillium promotes plant growth through auxin production. Rhizobium forms a symbiotic relationship with legume plants to fix nitrogen. Phosphate-Solubilizing Bacteria increase phosphorus availability. Cyanobacteria fix atmospheric nitrogen and produce growth-promoting substances. Other biofertilizer types may include mycorrhiza and plant growth-promoting rhizobacteria, among others. These biofertilizers offer sustainable agricultural solutions by reducing chemical fertilizer usage and improving soil health.</p></p>
