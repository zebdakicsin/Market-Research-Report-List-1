<p><p>The ultraviolet (UV) curable resin market comprises three major types: monomers, oligomers, and photoinitiators. Monomers are small molecules that react with other monomers to create long chains, forming the main structure of the resin. Oligomers are medium-sized molecules that add flexibility and strength to the resin, improving its overall performance. Photoinitiators are chemical compounds that initiate the curing process when exposed to UV light, triggering cross-linking reactions among monomers and oligomers, resulting in a solid and durable resin material. These components together contribute to the UV curable resin market's diverse range of applications.</p></p>
