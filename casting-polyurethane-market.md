<p><p>Casting polyurethane market types include polyester base prepolymer, polyether base prepolymer, and polycaprolactone base prepolymer. Polyester base prepolymer is known for its high mechanical strength and resistance to chemicals. Polyether base prepolymer is valued for its excellent flexibility, low temperature resistance, and superior hydrolytic stability. Polycaprolactone base prepolymer offers good elasticity, excellent adhesion, and enhanced tear resistance. These types of polyurethane are widely used in various industries such as automotive, construction, and electronics due to their unique properties and versatility in manufacturing different products.</p></p>
