<p><p>Pentane is an organic compound belonging to the alkane group, with a chemical formula of C5H12. It is a colorless liquid with a strong gasoline-like odor and is highly flammable. Pentane exists in three structural isomers: n-pentane, isopentane, and neopentane. It is primarily produced as a byproduct of crude oil refining and natural gas processing.</p><p>The pentane market has been witnessing significant growth in recent years, driven by its extensive use in various industries such as chemicals, energy, and pharmaceuticals. Pentane is commonly used as a blowing agent in the production of expanded polystyrene (EPS) foams, which are widely used in construction, packaging, and automotive industries. The growing construction sector, especially in developing regions, is contributing to the increased demand for pentane.</p><p>Additionally, the energy sector is a major consumer of pentane as it is used as a fuel in gasoline blends and as an aerosol propellant in various products such as automotive and household cleaners, paints, and personal care products.
