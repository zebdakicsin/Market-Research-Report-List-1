<p><p>The window motor market can be classified into two types: AC and DC. AC motors run on alternating current and are predominantly used in residential applications. These motors provide a steady and consistent torque, making them suitable for powering larger windows. On the other hand, DC motors run on direct current and are commonly found in automotive applications. They offer better control over speed and torque, making them ideal for operating car windows. The choice between AC and DC motors depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
