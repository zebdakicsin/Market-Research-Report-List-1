<p><p>Flame Retardant (FR) Composite Panels are categorized into different grades based on their fire resistance properties. </p><p>- A1 Grade panels have the highest level of fire resistance, offering non-combustible performance and producing no significant contribution to fire.</p><p>- A2 Grade panels are also non-combustible, but may release limited amounts of smoke during a fire.</p><p>- B Grade panels have limited fire resistance and can endure a small flame for a short period.</p><p>- C Grade panels have low fire resistance, easily igniting and contributing to the spread of fire. </p><p>These grades help users select the appropriate FR Composite Panel for their specific fire safety requirements.</p></p>
