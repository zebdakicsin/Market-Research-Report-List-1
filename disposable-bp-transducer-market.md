<p><ul><li>Single Line</li><li>Double Line</li><li>Triple Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Disposable BP Transducer market types refer to the number of pressure lines available within the transducer for measuring blood pressure. Single line transducers have one pressure line and are commonly used for monitoring systemic blood pressure. Double line transducers have two pressure lines and are often used for invasive pressure monitoring like arterial, venous, or central venous pressure. Triple line transducers have three pressure lines and are used for more comprehensive monitoring, including arterial, venous, and central venous pressure, providing a more complete picture of a patient's hemodynamic status.</p></p>
