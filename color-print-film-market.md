<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based Film</li><li>Water-Based Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are two main types of color print films in the market: solvent-based film and water-based film. Solvent-based film is a type of film that uses solvents as a base for the ink, enabling it to adhere to the print media effectively. This type of film offers vibrant and long-lasting colors. On the other hand, water-based film uses water as a base for the ink. It is considered an eco-friendly alternative to solvent-based film as it does not contain harmful chemicals. Water-based film also provides high-quality and durable color prints.</p></p>
