<p><p>Dissolved Oxygen Meters are used to measure the amount of oxygen dissolved in liquids, primarily in water. There are two main types of these meters: Polarographic Dissolved Oxygen Meters and Primary Battery Type Dissolved Oxygen Meters. Polarographic meters are based on the principle of oxygen reduction, using a polarizable electrode and a reference electrode. They require external power for operation. On the other hand, Primary Battery Type meters utilize a battery as their power source. Both types of meters are widely used in various industries and environmental monitoring applications.</p></p>
