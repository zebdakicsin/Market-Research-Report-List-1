<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The cabinet and enclosure heater market is classified into three types: PTC heaters, convection heaters, and fan heaters. PTC heaters utilize positive temperature coefficient elements that increase resistance with temperature, providing self-regulation and safety. Convection heaters rely on natural air circulation to produce heat and evenly distribute it within the enclosure. Fan heaters incorporate a fan to accelerate airflow, resulting in faster heating and better temperature balance. All these heater types cater to the specific requirements of different cabinet and enclosure heating applications.</p></p>
