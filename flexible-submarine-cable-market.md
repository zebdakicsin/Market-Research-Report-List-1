<p><p>Flexible submarine cables are used for transmitting electrical power and telecommunication signals over long distances underwater. There are two main types of cables in this market: impregnated paper insulated cables and oil-filled cables. Impregnated paper insulated cables consist of conductors insulated with impregnated paper and are mainly used for power transmission. On the other hand, oil-filled cables have conductors immersed in oil, which acts as both insulation and coolant, and are commonly deployed for telecommunication purposes. Both cable types enable efficient and reliable transmission of signals and power underwater.</p></p>
