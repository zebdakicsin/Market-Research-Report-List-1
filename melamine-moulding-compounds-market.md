<p><p>Melamine Moulding Compounds are used in two main types of molding processes: Injection Molding and Compression Molding. In Injection Molding, the melamine compound is melted and injected into a mold cavity under high pressure, allowing for the creation of complex shapes and smooth surfaces. Compression Molding involves placing the powdered melamine compound into a heated mold cavity and applying pressure to create the desired shape. Both processes are widely used in the production of melamine products, offering different advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
