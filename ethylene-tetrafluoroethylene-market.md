<p><p>The Ethylene Tetrafluoroethylene (ETFE) market consists of two types: ETFE Injection Molding and ETFE Extrusion Molding. ETFE Injection Molding involves the process of injecting the molten ETFE material into a mold to create various products. This method is commonly used for small and complex parts. On the other hand, ETFE Extrusion Molding involves melting the ETFE material and forcing it through a die to create long and continuous shapes, such as sheets or films. This method is often used for larger and simpler objects.</p></p>
