Single-use additives refer to chemicals added to provide specific properties or functions to a product, such as fragrance or color. Surfactants are compounds that enable the mixing of oil and water in cosmetic formulations. Thickening agents are substances that add viscosity and texture to cosmetic products, helping them to stay on the skin or hair.</p></p>
