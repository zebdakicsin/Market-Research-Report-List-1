Bluetooth technology enables short-range wireless communication, making it suitable for personal medical alert devices. Cellular technology provides wide coverage and allows emergency alerts to be transmitted using a mobile network. Other technologies may include proprietary wireless protocols or hybrid solutions combining multiple technologies.</p></p>
